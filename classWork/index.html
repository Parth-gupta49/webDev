- Steve Smith -

ABOUT

Steven Peter Devereux Smith (born 2 June 1989) is a British-Australian international cricketer and former captain of the Australian national team in all three formats of the game.

CAREER :
HEADLINES
  • Regarded as the best Test batsman of the modern era, as well as one of the greatest in the history of the sport
  • Smith has drawn comparisons to Don Bradman for his high Test batting average
  • Smith was a key member of the Australian teams that won the 2015 and 2023 Cricket World Cup, the 2021 ICC T20 World Cup, and the 2023 ICC World Test Championship final.
  • ICC All-Time batting ranking of No.2 (second only to the Don), playing style and is labelled 'the best since Bradman'
